# 📌 Business Insights 360 | Power BI Project

Business Insights 360 is an end-to-end business intelligence solution designed to provide a complete overview of company performance across Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive management. The dashboard helps organizations make strategic decisions using data-driven insights and interactive visualizations.

## 🎯 Key Objectives

* Analyze overall business performance and profitability
* Monitor sales growth and revenue trends
* Track customer and product performance
* Optimize supply chain and operational efficiency
* Enable executive-level decision-making using KPIs
* Identify growth opportunities and business risks

## 🌟 Dashboard Highlights

* Interactive business intelligence dashboard
* Real-time KPI tracking and performance monitoring
* Sales and revenue trend analysis
* Customer segmentation and product insights
* Region-wise and market-wise performance analysis
* Executive summary dashboard for strategic decisions
